Yummy Spinach

This is my easy go to healthy and delicous meal. I make it when I don't want have time to cook

Photo Credit: Our best bit

Ingredients

  • 4 tbs olive oil
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 1 bag of 16oz spinach
  • 1 tsp of all purpose seasoning
  • A pint of salt

Make the Spinach

  1. Turn the stove to medium heat
  2. Place a pan on the stove and let it heat up
  3. Pour the olive oil in the pan
  4. Satueed your onions in the olive oil until lightly brown
  5. Add your seasoning and the salt
  6. Sauteed for 2 minutes
  7. Spinach is ready, turn off the stove and move pan to cool down
